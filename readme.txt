== Description ==

Launch a digital goods store or your own marketplace with Checkout. Checkout is powered by Easy Digital Downloads, a free plugin that enables you to sell digital downloads with your WordPress site. Sell your stock photography, audio files, eBooks, video and more.

== Installation ==

1. Sign into your WordPress dashboard, go to Appearance > Themes, and click Add New.
2. Click Add New.
3. Click Upload.
4. Click Choose File and select the theme zip file you downloaded from Array.
5. Click Install Now.
6. Click Add New, then click Upload, then click Choose File.
7. After WordPress installs the theme, click Activate.
8. You've successfully installed your new theme!
